About Fallen Crown Records

Fallen Crown Records is a placeholder record label name I use when I upload my music to Distrokid.

FCR is a "real" label in that there is music out there with the FCR name, but FCR has no major corporate backing or anything.
It's just one guy making music, art, and other cool shit.


FCR is not seeking new artists or bands.

FCR does not do overly legally verbose contracts or any of that other corporate shit.

FCR is not looking to license audiovisuals for commercial use at this time.
