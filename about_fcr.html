About Fallen Crown Records

Fallen Crown Records is an independent record label owned by Hunter Wolfe.

FCR is a "real" label in that there is music out there with the FCR name, but FCR has no major corporate backing or anything.
We're just people making music.


Want your music published under FCR?
We offer fair and flexible publishing deals where you retain 100% control of your music. If you want to pack up and move to a different label,
we're not stopping you!
FCR is also open to licensing audiovisuals for commercial use. For more info, see our policy!
