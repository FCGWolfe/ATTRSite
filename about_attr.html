About ATTR

A Tear Through Reality is a solo music project that serves as a musical laboratory for Hunter Wolfe (that's me. hi! hello!)

ATTR can be described a few different ways:

"Solo Rock Project"
"Emotive Raprock Project"
"Trauma Project"
"Art Project"
"Some rando screaming into a mic while the world burns"

In any case, I make this music for me, usually to process shit going on in my life. I create what I want to see more of in the world.
Don't like it? Feel free to voice your disapproval so I can tell you where to shove it.
